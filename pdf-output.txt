Grade 3 Warehouse – Storage & Distribution  Services  Compliance, Safety, Cleaning, Induction & Training Manual  1. Purpose of This Document  This document outlines the operational, safety, hygiene, compliance, and training requirements for a  Grade 3 Storage & Distribution warehouse facility operating with:  Cold room storage  Forklift operations  3-level pallet racking  Pick packing operations  Dispatch and receiving  Staff amenities and lunchroom  Computer stations and warehouse administration  The purpose of this manual is to:  Maintain workplace safety  Meet warehouse compliance standards  Support audit readiness  Ensure food/product safety  Reduce workplace incidents  Train and induct all staff consistently  Maintain clean and organized warehouse operations  2.
